Privacy Policy

PawMap
Last updated: June 7, 2026

PawMap ("we", "our", or "us") operates the PawMap mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: email address and display name when you create an account.
  • Profile information: optional dog profile details and photos you choose to upload.
  • Location data: your device location to show nearby dog-friendly places, only with your permission.
  • User content: places, reviews, ratings, photos, and favorites you submit.
  • Usage data: app interactions collected through Firebase Analytics to improve performance and reliability.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core features such as maps, search, favorites, and account login.
  • Display community-submitted places and reviews.
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Respond to support requests.

Data Storage

Data is stored using Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, and Cloud Storage) on secure cloud infrastructure.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data may be processed by service providers such as Apple and Google/Firebase solely to operate the app.

Your Choices

  • You may deny location permission, though nearby search will be limited.
  • You may sign out of your account at any time.
  • You control what profile information and photos you upload.
  • To request account or data deletion, contact us using the email below.

Children

PawMap is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
